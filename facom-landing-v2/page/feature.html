Feature, Fee and Limit

  • Your FasaPay account limit are depend on your member status. Your Member status are displayed in the top corner of FasaPay Member Area beside your FasaPay account
  • The higher your Membership status (in the table bellow, right is higher than left) is the higher your limit.
  • The limit below is limit per transaction and not daily limit. You can do multiple transaction in the same day as long as each transaction are not exceeding the limit.
  • If you just registered to FasaPay, your first Membership status is Registered . Registered Member are only able to view their account and not allowed to do transaction.
  • To Upgrade your Membership, please upload required document using Upload ID page. The page can be accessed from Member Area > Profile > Upload ID
