Frequently Asked Questions - F.A.Q

Registration

Who can be a fasapay.com member?

Anyone can be a Fasapay.com member as long as they meet all the requirements.

How to register in fasapay.com?

Sign up as fasapay.com member at https://www.fasapay.com/daftar/langkahpertama/acctipe/personal​ . Fill thoroughly the form your personal data according your identity.

How to fill the account name if it consist of only one-word name?

If your name consists of only one word , please fill out the registration form with those one word

What are the identity requirements to verify fasapay.com account?

Fasapay requirements of the valid identity image to verify your fasapay.com account
  1. Valid International Passport.
  2. Use jpg. /jpeg. / png. format with colored image.
  3. Maximum image size is 1 MB.
  4. Four edges of the identity are clearly shown
  5. There must be a photograph of the ID owner
  6. Readable : Colored image, not too small, not blurry image, not edited/photo copied image.
  7. Upright (not tilted 90' or 180').
  8. There can be thin watermark on the image ID as long the data & photograph is still legible.
  9. There is page about the logo/symbol of the country or state regulations contained in the International passport.
  10. The validity period of identity image can not be less than 6 months.

How long it takes to get verified?

ID verification process is 24 hours / 1 (one) working day. The result of ID verification will be confirmed via email.

How to make fasapay.com account can be used for transactions?

  • After completed the registration process, please login to fasapay.com account https://www.fasapay.com/login.
  • Choose Profil menu >> Upload ID, to upload International Passport or Driver's License Image. You can see the requirements of the valid identity image on this link .
  • Wait until fasapay.com account is activated. After that go to Profil menu >> Bank, to filling the bank data.
  • fasapay.com account can be use for transactions after filling the bank data.

How to upgrade the fasapay.com account status?

  • To upgrade fasapay.com account status become VERIFIED, please upload your Billing Statement image in jpg / jpeg /png format ( Billing statement of Credit Card/electricity/Telephone/TV Cabel / Internet) through you fasapay account. Login > Profil > Upload ID.
  • To upgrade fasapay.com account status become CORPORATE / STORE, Please upload your original Certificate of Incorporations (colored and not a copied file /photocopy file) in english version. If you do not have your original Certificate of Incorporations in english, please kindly translate it to english version by official notary. Login > Profil > Upload ID.

Can I change the email address?

Email address can not be changed. Email address for fasapay.com account are bonded and not changeable. One email address is only valid for one account in fasapay.com. The registered email will be valid forever for one registered account.

Transaction

How to deposit in fasapay.com?

  • Top Up via USD bank account
    Before deposit in FasaPay, please fill deposit form thoroughly and accurately, our bank account number will be appear in the end of deposit form.
  • Top Up via IDR bank account / MasterChanger
    Generally, you need to do 2 steps:
    • First, Top UP to FI account : http://fasapay.co.id/service/topup
    • Second, Top UP your FP account

How to withdraw My USD/IDR (FP) balance to IDR (FI) balance?

Please be noted that this transaction is valid only from your FP account to your FI account.
From FP USD to FI
  1. Choose Transaction menu
  2. click Withdrawal submenu
  3. Select MasterChanger method.
      ​
    • Fill the Withdrawal Transaction Data
    • From Currency: USD
    • Amount Send: $ (minimum 1)
    • MasterChanger: Choose the MasterChanger
    • To FasaPay Indonesia Account: Choose your FI account
    • Note: add aditional note if necessary
    • Verification Code: enter the code correctly (Click image to refresh code if you want another code)
    • Click "Create" button (Please recheck to make sure you have entered correct numbers and accounts)
  4. Confirm Withdrawal Amounts
  5. Confirm Recipient/Destination Details
  6. Finish
  7. Go to your Fasapay.co.id account and the IDR balance will immediately credited.
From FP IDR to FI
  1. Choose Transaction menu
  2. click Withdrawal submenu
  3. Select MasterChanger method.
      ​
    • Fill the Withdrawal Transaction Data
    • From Currency: IDR
    • Amount Send: Rp (minimum 1000)
    • MasterChanger: Choose Epoin
    • To FasaPay Indonesia Account: Choose your FI account
    • Note: add aditional note if necessary
    • Verification Code: enter the code correctly (Click image to refresh code if you want another code)
    • Click "Create" button (Please recheck to make sure you have entered correct numbers and accounts)
  4. Confirm Withdrawal Amounts
  5. Confirm Recipient/Destination Details
  6. Finish
  7. Go to your Fasapay.co.id account and the IDR balance will immediately credited

How to redeem/withdrawal in fasapay.com?

Redeem/withdraw to USD bank account
  1. Login to member area https://www.fasapay.com/login.
  2. Choose Transaction > Withdrawal > Bank Transfer.
  3. Fill withdrawal form thoroughly and accurately.
  4. Choose your currency (USD).
  5. Choose your bank you will use to received the fund from your fasapay.com account.
Redeem to IDR bank account
  1. Go to http://www.fasapay.com and log in to member area.
  2. Choose Transaction menu and click Withdrawal submenu, select MasterChanger Method.
  3. Next, you will be taken to a deposit form page where you need to finish all the withdrawal steps.
  4. Fill the withdrawal transaction data
    • From Currency * (choose USD if you want Withdraw USD currency, choose IDR if you want IDR currency)
    • Amount Send * (fill the ammount according to the ammount of money you will transfer to your FI account)
    • MasterChanger (choose any MasterChanger available)
    • Read the Exchange Rate and you can continue.
    • To FasaPay Indonesia Account * (Choose your FI account number)
    • Add Note if necessary
    • Enter Verification Code
  5. Click "CREATE" and In the next step you will be asked to send your fund from your FP account to FPxxx (FP MasterChanger account).
  6. After that you will receive Rpxxx in your FI account From FIxxx (FI MasterChanger account).
  7. Check/Tick (V) the “Agreement Button” then you will be shown the Confirm Transaction Detail.
  8. Check/Tick (V) the “Agreement Button” and then click "New Transaction Confirmation" button.
  9. Finish.

How to add another bank account which is unregistered in bank list?

Choose other bank (bottom), then fill the form correctly (bank name, registration address, swift code (you can check on the link available), detail, account, name, verification code)

Must I fill the transaction form everytime I deposit?

Corect, user/member must fill transaction form to deposit. For additional information, one single transaction form is valid for only one fund transfer. Example : if you fill 2 deposit forms with US$1000.00 and US$ 5000.00 amount, you should send the funds twice according to the deposit form.

How long does the deposit take in fasapay.com?

Deposit transaction in fasapay.com will be processed on our services hours https://www.fasapay.com/about/officehour according to the incoming transaction queue and will take time maximum 60 minutes after fasapay.com received the funds and deposit form TUxxx.

How much the transaction fee in fasapay.com?

How much the transaction fee in fasapay.com? There is no charge or fee on FasaPay for deposit and withdraw transaction via bank transfer, but there will be an administrative fees and transactions from the bank. Transaction fees are only charged on Fund Transfer transactions between FasaPay accounts (FP to FP) and MasterChanger transactions (IDR to USD or USD to IDR). The fee of the transaction is 0.5% of the transaction value with a maximum fee of $ 5.00 or Rp. 50.000,00

Merchant

How much do we have to pay to join as FasaPay Merchant?

  • FasaPay registration fee as merchant is free/no charge.
  • FasaPay fee transfer is 0.5% from total transaction with maximal fee IDR 50.000/ US$5,00.
  • FasaPay has no monthly fee, annual or hidden fee.

How to be FasaPay Merchant?

Become FasaPay merchant with CORPORATE status
  1. Sign up as FasaPay member at https://www.fasapay.com/
  2. Upload Personal in Charge of fasapay account international passport along with upper part image (State Emblem/regulation part) : Login > Profile > Upload ID.
  3. Update your bank account : Login > Profile > Bank > Add Bank
  4. Upload your company statement letter that stating the Personal in Charge of FasaPay account is your company's employees. Complete the statement letter with company logo, authorized signature and company stamp : Login > Profile > Upload ID.
  5. Upgrade your membership status from ACTIVE to CORPORATE by uploading document scans of Certificate of Incorporations : Login > Profile > Upload ID.
  6. Integrate FasaPay SCI into your website. The guide about this SCI usage is provided via member page, You can check this link for more information
    • https://www.fasapay.com/en/sciguide/index
    • https://www.fasapay.com/en/apiguide/index
    • https://www.fasapay.com/en/sciguide/sandbox (for transaction testing)
Since this procedure is somewhat technical matter, you can reach our IT Support available for you at technical@fasapay.com .

If FasaPay SCI has been successfully integrated into your website, please send a confirmation and send your company logo to us so that we can immediately display your company logo on FasaPay website on the Merchant Page.

What is Store and Corporate status?

There are two type of merchant in FasaPay, Store and Corporate. Store for personal bussiness and Corporate for company bussiness. Both have a different requirement.

Requirement document for Store : Company Owner Passport and Company legality. Requirement document for Corporate : Company Representative Passport and Company legality.

Upload the requirement document in member area menu Profile > Upload ID
